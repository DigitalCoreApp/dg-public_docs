# AI capabilities

DigitalCore is organised around the natural rhythm of service management. Each capability covers one step in the cycle, so you always know which to use.

## The seven capabilities

| Capability | What it does |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Monitor** | Health snapshots for portfolios and engagements. |
| **Record** | Log hours, finance actuals, and KPI values in natural language. |
| **Diagnose** | Variance analysis and showback by department or partner. |
| **Plan** | Generate and refine response options for a decision. |
| **Decide** | Compare options and apply the chosen one to your plan. |
| **Learn** | Explore KPI definitions, playbooks, and benchmarks. |
| **Communicate** | Period summaries and executive reports for your stakeholders. |

## Monitor

Ask for the current health of a portfolio or engagement. You get the score, domain breakdown, top concerns, and trend without opening multiple screens.

## Record

Say "Record 160 hours for Senior Consultant on the Acme engagement for January." DigitalCore confirms the entry and writes it. Automatic labour cost and penalty calculations apply just like manual entry.

## Diagnose

Ask for a showback report or variance breakdown. Costs are split by delivery party so you can see which department, customer, or partner carries each share.

## Plan

Describe the situation ("margins on Contoso are slipping, what are my options?") and DigitalCore generates 2–4 response options with projected impact. Refine with conversation: "make the cost cut deeper" or "add a 5% price increase."

## Decide

Compare options side by side. When you pick one, **Approve & Apply** writes its values into the live plan. You can reverse this if you change your mind.

## Learn

Look up any KPI for the formula, target ranges, and improvement tips. Browse the 29 service management playbooks, or run a quick health check from a few headline numbers.

## Communicate

Generate a period summary for a chosen audience, or build a multi‑section executive report. Edit, approve, and export as PDF.
