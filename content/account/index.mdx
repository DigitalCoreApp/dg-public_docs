# Account and Billing

Your personal account settings and your organisation''s subscription.

## In this section

- [Profile Settings](/account/profile) - your name, email, and notification preferences.
- [Security](/account/security) - password and two-factor authentication.
- [Subscription](/account/subscription) - plan, seats, and invoices.
- [Pricing](/account/pricing) - current plan options.

## Roles vs account

- **Account settings** are personal (your profile and security).
- **Subscription** is per organisation (one billing record).
- **Roles** are per organisation member and live under [Organization, Roles and Permissions](/organization/roles).
