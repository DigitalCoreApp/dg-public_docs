# Managing Your Subscription

The Subscription page is for organisation administrators. Open it from the user menu under **Subscription**.

## What you see

- **Current plan** and renewal date.
- **Seats** used and total available.
- **Engagements** in use.
- **Invoices** for the past 12 months.

## Change plan

Click **Change Plan**. Pick a new plan and confirm.

- Upgrades take effect immediately. The new rate is prorated.
- Downgrades take effect at the next renewal.

## Add or remove seats

Click **Manage Seats**. Seats are billed monthly.

## Update billing details

Click **Billing Details** to change your card or billing address.

## Cancel

Click **Cancel Plan**. Cancellation takes effect at the next renewal. Your data stays available until then.

## Invoices

Each invoice can be downloaded as PDF.
