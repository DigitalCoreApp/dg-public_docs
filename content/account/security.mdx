# Security

Settings to keep your account safe.

## Password

- At least 12 characters.
- Mix of letters, numbers, and a symbol.
- Cannot match your last 5 passwords.

Click **Change Password** to update it.

## Two-factor authentication

Two-factor (2FA) adds a second step at sign-in.

1. Go to **Profile**, then **Security**.
2. Click **Enable 2FA**.
3. Scan the QR code with an authenticator app (1Password, Authy, Google Authenticator).
4. Enter the 6-digit code to confirm.

Save the backup codes somewhere safe. You will need them if you lose your phone.

## Active sessions

Under **Active Sessions** you can see where you are signed in and sign out other devices.

## Sign-in alerts

Email alerts on new device sign-ins are on by default. Turn them off in notification preferences.

## Tips

- Use a password manager.
- Turn on 2FA, even on small accounts.
- Sign out devices you no longer use.
