# Pricing

For up-to-date pricing, plan names, and feature comparison, visit [digitalcore.app/pricing](https://digitalcore.app/pricing).

## What you get on every plan

- Engagements, contracts, and templates.
- Monthly check-ins.
- Pulse and Portfolio dashboards.
- Decisions and Responses.
- Executive summaries.
- The AI assistant.

## What changes by plan

- Number of users.
- Number of engagements.
- API access.
- Integration access (Slack, Microsoft Teams, QuickBooks).
- Support level.

## Trials

A 14-day trial is available on paid plans. No credit card required to start.

## Change your plan

Open [Subscription](/account/subscription) to upgrade, downgrade, or cancel.
