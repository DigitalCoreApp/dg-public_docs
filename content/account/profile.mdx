# Profile Settings

Open the user menu in the top right and click **Profile**.

## What you can edit

- **Name.** Shown in activity logs and reports.
- **Email.** Used to sign in and to receive notifications.
- **Avatar.** Shown next to your name.
- **Time zone.** Affects when scheduled reports run.
- **Language.** Display language for the app.

## Notification preferences

Choose which events email you:

- Open Decisions assigned to you.
- Missed SLA terms on engagements you own.
- Scheduled reports.
- Mentions in comments.

You can also turn off email entirely. In-app notifications stay on.

## Change your password

Click **Change Password** in the Security section. You will be asked for your current password.
