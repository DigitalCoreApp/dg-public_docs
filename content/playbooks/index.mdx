# Playbooks

Playbooks are short response guides. Each one covers a single problem (margin loss, capacity gap, missed SLA term, renewal risk) and gives a practical sequence of steps.

## Where you see them

- In the AI assistant when a problem is detected.
- On a Decision, when a matching playbook applies.
- On the engagement page, under **Playbooks**.

## What is in a playbook

- **Trigger.** The signal that surfaces the playbook.
- **Diagnosis.** Questions to confirm the problem.
- **Actions.** Steps to take, in order.
- **Outcomes.** What success looks like.

## Examples

- Margin Recovery
- Capacity Rebalance
- SLA Breach Response
- Renewal Risk
- Cost Spike Diagnosis
- Pricing Review

## Use a playbook

1. Open the playbook from the engagement or Decision.
2. Tick off the steps as you go.
3. Convert any step into a Decision if the action needs sign-off.

## Bring your own

Custom playbooks are on the roadmap. For now, edit the default ones in **Configure**, then **Playbooks**.
