# Strategy and Priorities

The Strategy view links your priorities to the engagements that deliver them. It is read-mostly and updated when priorities change.

## Priorities

A Priority is a goal worth tracking. Examples: "Lift portfolio margin to 35%", "Reduce missed SLA terms by half", "Land three new managed services contracts".

## Link engagements

Each priority lists the engagements that contribute. The engagement health rolls up into a single colour for the priority.

## Add a Priority

1. Open **Strategy** and click **New Priority**.
2. Give it a title and target.
3. Pick the engagements it covers.

## Track progress

The Priority page shows:

- Aggregate health across linked engagements.
- Trend over the last three periods.
- Open Decisions tagged to the priority.

## Approve a Response from here

You can open a Response from a Priority, compare options across engagements, and apply.
