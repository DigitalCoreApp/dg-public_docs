# Using Governance and Decisions

Decisions are how you record important choices and track them through to outcome. They live in the Decisions workspace.

## When to open a Decision

- A material change in plan or scope.
- A response to a missed SLA term.
- A pricing or staffing change worth recording.
- A risk you want owned and tracked.

## Open one

1. Go to **Decisions** and click **New Decision**.
2. Pick the type (Plan change, Risk mitigation, Pricing, Staffing, Other).
3. Link it to an engagement and, if useful, to a Response.

## Capture the case

- **Context.** What is happening and why it matters.
- **Options.** What you are choosing between.
- **Recommendation.** Your preferred path and reasoning.
- **Owner.** Who decides.

## Move it through

Statuses progress: Proposed, Approved, Declined, Applied. Each step writes to the activity log.

## Outcome

After the period closes, record the outcome on the Decision. This builds a track record you can review later.
