# Creating and Comparing Responses

A Response is how you frame a problem, compare options, and apply a plan change. Responses live on an engagement.

## Frame the problem

1. Open the engagement and click **New Response**.
2. Give it a title (for example, "Recover Acme margin").
3. Pick the period the response covers.

## Build options

Each Response can have several Options. An Option is one possible plan.

1. Click **Add Option**.
2. Edit the assumptions: rates, hours, KPIs, prices.
3. DigitalCore recalculates the engagement plan in place.

You can ask the assistant to generate options. It proposes three by default, each with a different trade-off.

## Compare side by side

Open the **Compare** view. Each option shows margin, cost, capacity, and risk for the period.

## Approve and apply

When you have picked an option:

1. Click **Approve & Apply**.
2. The chosen option becomes the new plan for the period.
3. Other options are kept as Declined for the activity log.

## Statuses

- **Proposed.** Draft, not visible to others.
- **Approved.** Selected as the chosen path.
- **Declined.** Considered and not chosen.
- **Applied.** Pushed into the live plan.
