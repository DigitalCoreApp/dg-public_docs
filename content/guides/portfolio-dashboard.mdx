# Reading the Portfolio Dashboard

The Portfolio dashboard shows every engagement in one view, sorted by health and exposure.

## What you see

- **Health column.** Green, Amber, or Red, one per engagement.
- **Margin column.** Plan vs actual for the current period.
- **Trend.** Direction over the last three periods.
- **Open items.** Number of open decisions, missed terms, and risks.

## Filters

- **Period.** Single month, quarter, or year-to-date.
- **Owner.** Filter by delivery lead or account manager.
- **Service.** Group by service line.

## Drill in

Click any engagement to open its detail page. From there you can run a check-in, open a decision, or build a response.

## Sort by exposure

Switch the sort to **Exposure** to surface the engagements with the largest plan-vs-actual gap, regardless of health colour.

## Tips

- Save a personal view by setting filters and clicking **Save view**.
- Star engagements you own. Starred engagements appear at the top of Pulse.
