# FAQ

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

## What is DigitalCore?

A platform for service businesses to see the financial health of every engagement in real time. See [What is DigitalCore](/).

## Who is it for?

Managed service providers, consultancies, IT service desks, agencies, and NGOs running services. People who use it: operations, finance, delivery, and service leads.

## How long does setup take?

A small team can be running their first month within a day. Importing past data takes longer and depends on how much you bring in.

## Do I need a finance system to start?

No. You can record actuals manually each month. If you have QuickBooks, you can connect it to pull invoices and expenses automatically.

## Where does the data live?

In DigitalCore''s hosted database. Each customer''s data is isolated. We do not share data between customers.

## Can I export my data?

Yes. Export to CSV from any view, or to PDF for reports.

## Is there a free trial?

Yes, 14 days on paid plans. No credit card needed.

## Does DigitalCore replace my finance system?

No. It complements it. Your finance system stays the source of truth for accounting. DigitalCore is the source of truth for service economics.

## What is the AI assistant?

A chat in the app and on connected AI tools. It answers questions, drafts summaries, and helps build response options. See [Using the AI Assistant](/guides/ai-assistant).

## Can I use DigitalCore from Claude or ChatGPT?

Yes. See [Connections and Integrations](/mcp-and-integrations).
