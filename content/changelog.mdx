# Changelog

Recent updates to DigitalCore.

## April 2026

- AI Usage is now a first-class data domain alongside Finance, Performance, and Capacity. Track tokens, model spend, and ROI per engagement.
- ServiceNow integration: pull incidents, problems, and changes into Performance, with auto-link to engagements.
- Microsoft Teams notifications: install the DigitalCore Teams app once, then connect any channel with a one-time registration code.
- Email distribution lists: send reports and alerts to managed recipient lists, not just individual addresses.
- Slack OAuth flow simplified: no admin-approval step in the docs path.

## March 2026

- Automated risk detection: data uploads and check-ins now scan for cross-domain signals (margin pressure, cost escalation, SLA breach, capacity overrun) and create governance cases for critical issues.
- Intelligence pages: Risk Exposure, Utilization Loss, Delivery Cost, and Correlations.
- QuickBooks sync extended to cover invoice and expense categories.

## February 2026

- AI assistant connections (MCP) now generally available, both free and paid.
- Microsoft Teams notifications.
- Faster Portfolio dashboard.

## January 2026

- New version of Responses and Decisions.
- Refreshed Pulse, Portfolio, Engagement, and Communicate sections.
- Strategy view with Priorities.

## December 2025

- Executive summaries with scheduled delivery.
- Slack notifications.
- QuickBooks invoice and expense sync.

## November 2025

- Excel and CSV import.
- Capacity hours auto-post to Finance.
- SLA term breach detection.

## October 2025

- Initial public release.
