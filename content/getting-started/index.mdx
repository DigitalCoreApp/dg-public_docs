# Quickstart Overview

Go from a blank account to your first check-in in about 30 minutes. No install. No credit card.

## What you'll set up

By the end you will have:

- An organisation with at least one team member.
- One service with an active engagement.
- Templates that track finance, performance, and capacity.
- One month of actuals entered.
- A Pulse view showing the engagement's health.

## Six steps

| Step | What you do | Page |
|------|-------------|------|
| 1 | Create your organisation, confirm email, invite users. | [Create Your Organisation](/getting-started/organisation) |
| 2 | Add a service: the thing you deliver. | [Services, Engagements & Contracts](/getting-started/core-entities) |
| 3 | Add an engagement under that service for a customer. | [Services, Engagements & Contracts](/getting-started/core-entities) |
| 4 | Pick templates for finance, performance, and capacity. | [Configure Your Templates](/getting-started/templates) |
| 5 | Enter the first month of actuals. | [Your First Check-in](/getting-started/first-checkin) |
| 6 | Read your Pulse and engagement health. | [Reading Your Dashboard](/getting-started/dashboard) |

## Optional next steps

- Add a contract with rate cards and SLA terms.
- Group services into portfolios.
- Backfill history with [Excel / CSV import](/mcp-and-integrations/excel-import).
- Stream live data from [QuickBooks](/mcp-and-integrations/quickbooks) or [ServiceNow](/mcp-and-integrations/servicenow).
- Connect Claude, Cursor, or ChatGPT through the [DigitalCore MCP](/mcp-and-integrations/paid-mcp).
- Try [scenarios](/guides/scenarios) and [executive summaries](/guides/executive-summaries).
