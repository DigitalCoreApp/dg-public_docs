# Create Your Organisation

Create an account, set up your organisation, and invite users. About 5 minutes.

## Sign up

1. Go to [digitalcore.app](https://digitalcore.app) and click **Start Free**.
2. Enter your email and a password.
3. Click the confirmation link in your inbox.

New accounts start on the free tier. You can upgrade later from [Account: Subscription](/account/subscription).

## Name your organisation

After your first sign-in you name the organisation. This is the company or entity that owns the data. Your organisation's data is private to your users.

Optional setup:

- **Structure**: add units or departments to group services and reports.
- **Partners**: add external customers and delivery partners under Configure. Use them as parties on engagements.

## Invite your team

Go to **Organization → Users** and click **Invite User**. Pick a role and enter the email.

| Role | Can do |
|------|--------|
| Admin | Everything: users, settings, templates, library, all data. |
| Manager | Edit engagements, run responses, send reports. No org settings. |
| Operator | Enter check-in data. View Pulse and reports. |
| Viewer | Read-only across Pulse, reports, and engagements. |

Invitees land on Pulse on first sign-in. What they can click depends on the role.

## Resending or cancelling invites

Unaccepted invites are listed on the Users page. Click **Resend** or **Cancel** at any time.
