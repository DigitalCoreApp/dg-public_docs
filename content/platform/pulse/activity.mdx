# Activity

Activity is a live feed of every action across your organisation, by people and by DigitalCore. Use it to check what just happened, review recent automation, or spot anything unexpected.

## What you will see

| Event | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| Signal detected | DigitalCore noticed a pattern worth flagging. |
| Response proposed | Options were generated for a signal or case. |
| Response applied | An option was approved and applied. |
| Recording made | Hours, finance, KPI, or AI usage was logged. |
| Case opened, decided, or closed | A decision case changed state. |
| Report sent | A report was finalised and shared. |
| Limit crossed | A configured threshold was breached. |
| Policy ran | A policy rule allowed, blocked, or held an action. |

## Filters

Filter the feed by:

- **Who.** User, the assistant, or the system.
- **Engagement.** Limit to one engagement.
- **Event type.** Any of the rows above.
- **Severity.** Critical, High, Medium, or Low.
- **Time range.** Last hour, 24 hours, 7 days, or a custom range.

## Activity vs History

Activity is the live, everything-included feed. [History](/platform/pulse/history) is the permanent record of decisions and applied responses, kept for audit and review.
