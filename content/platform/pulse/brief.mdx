# Daily Brief

The Daily Brief is a plain-English summary of what changed across your portfolio since you last logged in. It is written from your live data, not a generic template.

## What you will see

A typical brief covers four things:

- **Headline.** The single most important change. For example, "Acme Migration moved to Red on margin."
- **What changed.** Health scores that shifted colour, new signals, cases opened.
- **Already handled.** Responses that were approved or applied since your last visit.
- **Needs your attention.** Items you can find in [My Work](/platform/pulse/my-work).

If nothing meaningful happened, the brief stays quiet.

## When it updates

The brief refreshes whenever the underlying data changes by enough to matter. There is no fixed schedule.

## What controls the brief

Two screens shape what the brief includes and how it sounds:

- [Thresholds](/platform/configure/intelligence/thresholds) decide what counts as worth mentioning.
- [Policies](/platform/configure/intelligence/policies) decide what DigitalCore handles for you, which is reflected in the "already handled" section.

Report audience (Internal, Customer, Executive, Board) is set in [Reports](/platform/communicate/reports).
