# Overview

The Overview tab is a one-screen snapshot of where things stand. Use it when you want numbers at a glance instead of the written [Daily Brief](/platform/pulse/brief).

## Top numbers

| Number | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Active Services | Services your organisation is managing right now. |
| Due This Week | Check-ins that should be completed this week. |
| Overdue | Check-ins past their due date. |

## Cards on the page

**Attention Required.** Items that need a response: overdue check-ins, engagements with falling health, recent SLA breaches, upcoming contract renewals. Each link opens the relevant engagement.

**Portfolio Health.** A 0 to 100 score with sub-scores for Finance, Performance, Capacity, and AI Usage. The colour (Green, Amber, or Red) gives a quick read. Bands are set in [Thresholds](/platform/configure/intelligence/thresholds).

**Workspace Launchers.** Tiles that take you to [Engagements](/platform/engagements), [Portfolio](/platform/portfolio), [Decisions](/platform/decisions), and [Communicate](/platform/communicate).

## Onboarding checklist

New organisations see a setup checklist on this tab:

1. Create your first service.
2. Set up an engagement.
3. Assign templates.
4. Run your first check-in.

The checklist hides once steps are complete or dismissed.
