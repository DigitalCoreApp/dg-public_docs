# History

History is the long-term record of decisions made, responses applied, and signals closed. [Activity](/platform/pulse/activity) shows the live feed. History is what you keep for the record.

## What is recorded

| Entry | What it includes |
|-------|------------------|
| Decision | The case, the options considered, the option chosen, the reason, and who decided. |
| Applied response | The option that was applied, the assumptions behind it, and the projected impact. |
| Signal closed | When and how a signal was resolved. |
| Case closed | Final state and outcome notes. |
| Reverted response | Responses that were applied then rolled back. |

Entries cannot be edited or deleted. You can add notes later, but the original record stays.

## Why History is separate from Activity

1. **Record-keeping.** Auditors and clients can see exactly when, why, and by whom each decision was made.
2. **Outcome review.** Decisions can be linked to measured results weeks or months later. See [Decisions: Cases](/platform/decisions/cases).
3. **Learning.** Real outcomes feed back into DigitalCore's pattern recognition over time. See [Specialist Memory](/platform/configure/intelligence/ai-agents/specialist-memory).

## Search and filters

- Engagement or portfolio.
- Decided by (user or DigitalCore).
- Outcome (decided, applied, reverted, closed).
- Date range.
- Free-text search over rationale and notes.
