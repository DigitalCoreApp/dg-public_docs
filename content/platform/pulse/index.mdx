# Pulse

Pulse is the first screen you see when you log in. It answers one question: what should I do right now?

Most users start every day here.

## The six tabs

| Tab | What you see |
|-----|--------------|
| [Daily Brief](/platform/pulse/brief) | A short write-up of what changed since your last visit. |
| [Signals](/platform/pulse/signals) | Issues DigitalCore noticed: margin pressure, SLA risk, capacity overrun, and similar. |
| [My Work](/platform/pulse/my-work) | Items waiting on you: approvals, cases you own, overdue check-ins. |
| [Overview](/platform/pulse/overview) | A snapshot dashboard with key numbers and portfolio health. |
| [Activity](/platform/pulse/activity) | A live feed of everything happening across the organisation. |
| [History](/platform/pulse/history) | The permanent record of decisions and applied responses. |
