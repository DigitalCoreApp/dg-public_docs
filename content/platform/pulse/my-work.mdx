# My Work

My Work shows items that need you specifically. Not your team, not the system. Just you.

## What shows up here

| Item | Why it appears |
|------|----------------|
| Approvals waiting | DigitalCore proposed a response and you need to approve or decline it. |
| Cases you own | Decision cases assigned to you. |
| Overdue check-ins | Engagements you operate where a check-in is late. |
| Notifications | High-priority alerts routed to you. |
| Mentions | Comments where someone tagged you. |

Items disappear once you act on them: approve, decline, complete, or dismiss.

## Why this list exists

DigitalCore handles a lot for you in the background. Anything it can do safely under your [Policies](/platform/configure/intelligence/policies) is done automatically and logged in [Activity](/platform/pulse/activity). Anything that needs your judgement lands here.

## Bulk actions

Select several items at once to:

- Approve and apply them together (when they are the same type).
- Decline with a reason.
- Reassign to another user.
- Snooze for a set number of days.
