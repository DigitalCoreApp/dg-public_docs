# Engagement Analytics

Analytics is the deep view of a single engagement. The engagement detail page shows where things stand today. Analytics shows how they got there and how the different areas relate.

## What you see

| Section | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| Trend chart | Up to 24 months of revenue, cost, margin, hours, and KPI achievement. |
| Cross-area heatmap | Where capacity, performance, and finance move together. |
| Anomalies | Outliers compared to recent history. |
| Written summary | A short explanation of what changed and why. |
| Comparable engagements | Similar engagements for benchmarking. |

## Common uses

- **Renewal preparation.** What does the data say going into the conversation?
- **Quarterly business reviews.** What three things does the customer need to know?
- **Retrospective.** Which patterns predicted the issues you hit?
- **Pricing review.** Does this engagement carry its weight?

## How the written summary is produced

The summary blends rule-based facts (always available) with DigitalCore-generated commentary (when AI agents are switched on). The generated version draws on what your team has accepted in past summaries so the phrasing matches house style.
