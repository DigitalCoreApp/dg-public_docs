# Strategic Hub

The Strategic Hub is the one screen for leaders who do not have time to dig. Open it, read the headline. If something needs attention, click through.

## Headline tiles

| Tile | What it shows | Drill-down |
|------|---------------|------------|
| Portfolio health | Weighted average health score across engagements. | [Engagements](/platform/engagements) |
| Margin | Margin percent with the week-on-week or month-on-month arrow. | [Service Economics](/platform/portfolio/service-economics) |
| Utilisation | Planned versus actual hours and the leakage percent. | [Utilization Loss](/platform/portfolio/utilization-loss) |
| Open risks | Count by severity. | [Risk Exposure](/platform/portfolio/risk-exposure) |
| SLA achievement | Percent of KPIs at or above target. | [Service Economics](/platform/portfolio/service-economics) |
| AI spend | Token cost month to date against budget. | [Engagements](/platform/engagements) |

## Top movers

Below the tiles you see:

- Top five health drops this period.
- Top five margin pressures.
- Top five demand signals (services running hot on capacity).

Each row is one click into the engagement.

## Filters

| Filter | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Period | Default rolling 30 days. Switch to MTD, QTD, YTD, or custom. |
| Portfolio | Scope to one or more portfolios. |
| Tag | Free-form scoping by [service tag](/platform/configure/service-setup/service-tags). |
| Service line | Restrict to one or more services. |

Selected filters carry across the detail pages. Pick once, drill anywhere.
