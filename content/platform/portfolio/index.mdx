# Portfolio

Portfolio is for leaders who need to see the whole book at once. [Engagements](/platform/engagements) lets you go deep on one delivery. Portfolio rolls up across all of them so you can spot patterns instead of single-project noise.

## What you will find

| Page | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| [Strategic Hub](/platform/portfolio/strategic-hub) | One screen with the headline numbers. |
| [Service Economics](/platform/portfolio/service-economics) | Margin and revenue trends across services and portfolios. |
| [Delivery Cost](/platform/portfolio/delivery-cost) | Total cost of delivery by layer: labour, vendor, infrastructure, SLA, overhead. |
| [Utilization Loss](/platform/portfolio/utilization-loss) | Where capacity is leaking and why. |
| [Risk Exposure](/platform/portfolio/risk-exposure) | SLA penalties, risk register, and demand signals. |
| [Correlations](/platform/portfolio/correlations) | Patterns across finance, performance, capacity, and AI usage. |
| [Compare](/platform/portfolio/compare) | Engagements side by side. |

## How the totals work

Portfolio numbers are totals across:

- All [services](/platform/configure/service-setup/services) you have access to.
- The [portfolio](/platform/configure/service-setup/portfolios) or [tag](/platform/configure/service-setup/service-tags) filter you select.
- The selected period: usually last full month, last quarter, or year to date.

Totals refresh after each recording or signal, with a short cooldown so the page does not thrash.

## Who uses Portfolio

| Role | Typical use |
|------|-------------|
| Service owner or practice lead | Daily glance at health and risks across the book. |
| CFO or finance | Margin trends, cost stack, currency-converted P&L. |
| CEO or executive | Strategic Hub for the headline, Compare for benchmarking. |
| PMO | Utilisation loss, demand signals, capacity planning. |
