# Delivery Cost

Total Cost of Delivery is the full picture of what it costs to deliver each service. Most organisations only see labour. DigitalCore shows the full stack.

## The five cost layers

| Layer | Where the value comes from | How it is added |
|-------|----------------------------|-----------------|
| Labour | Hours times rate. | Posted automatically when hours are recorded. |
| Vendor or partner | Recorded as finance actuals. | Manually or through QuickBooks sync. |
| Infrastructure | Recorded as finance actuals. | Manually or through QuickBooks sync. |
| SLA penalties | KPI breaches. | Posted automatically when a breach is detected. |
| Overhead | Allocated through a finance template. | Manually or on a schedule. |

Labour and SLA penalties are added for you. The other three come from finance recording or the QuickBooks integration.

## Where to use this view

- **Renewal pricing.** What is the floor below which you lose money?
- **Margin protection.** Which cost layer is moving the wrong way?
- **Vendor reviews.** Is partner or sub spend justified by the margin?
- **SLA design.** Is the penalty risk worth the contract value?
