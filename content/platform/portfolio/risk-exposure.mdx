# Risk Exposure

Risk Exposure is the unified read on what could hurt margin or delivery in the near term. It blends three sources.

| Source | What it represents |
|--------|--------------------|
| SLA penalties | Realised and projected penalty cost from KPI breaches. |
| Risk register | Active risks logged by people or auto-created from [signals](/platform/pulse/signals). |
| Demand signals | Capacity, pipeline, or margin signals trending toward critical. |

## The headline number

Total exposure equals realised penalties plus risk register monetary impact plus projected demand-driven cost. This is the if-nothing-changes cost number.

## Breakdown

| Section | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| By severity | Critical, High, Medium, Low. |
| By engagement | Top 10 most exposed engagements. |
| By type | SLA, register, demand-driven. |
| Trend | Exposure over the last six months. |

## Risks DigitalCore creates for you

When DigitalCore detects a critical signal it can:

1. Open a [decision case](/platform/decisions/cases) of type risk mitigation.
2. Add an entry to the risk register.
3. Notify engagement owners through [Pulse](/platform/pulse) and your [delivery channels](/platform/configure/intelligence/ai-agents/delivery-channels).

Auto-created risks show up here next to ones logged by hand.
