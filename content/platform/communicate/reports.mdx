# Reports

Reports is the scheduling and delivery hub for periodic communication. It covers everything from a Monday-morning portfolio summary to a quarterly executive brief.

## Report types

| Type | Use | Default cadence |
|------|-----|-----------------|
| Period summary | Last week or last month rollup of one engagement or portfolio. | Weekly or monthly. |
| Executive brief | Cross-portfolio summary, top movers, top risks. | Monthly or quarterly. |
| QBR deck | Quarterly business review, slide-ready. | Quarterly. |
| Customer report | Shared via [client portal](/platform/communicate/client-portals). | Monthly. |
| One-off | Generated on demand. | n/a |

## Lifecycle

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Draft | Generated, not yet finalised. |
| In review | Sent to a reviewer for sign-off. |
| Finalised | Locked, PDF rendered, ready to distribute. |
| Distributed | Sent through the configured channels. |

The draft to review to finalise to distribute flow follows the same [autonomy](/platform/configure/intelligence/ai-agents/agent-profiles) and [policies](/platform/configure/intelligence/policies) as any other automated output.

## Editor

Reports open in a structured editor:

- Sections (title, summary text, charts, tables) you can drag to reorder.
- Live data binding: sections update if data changes, until the report is finalised.
- Manual edits: override anything DigitalCore wrote.
- Templates: start from a saved template, or save your own.

## Distribution

When the report is finalised it is delivered through the [notification channels](/platform/communicate/notification-channels) set up for its audience. PDF copies are stored alongside the record so you can audit or resend.
