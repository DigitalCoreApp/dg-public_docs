# Notification Channels

Notification Channels are how DigitalCore delivers signals, reports, and other messages. The set-up lives in [Configure → AI Agents → Delivery Channels](/platform/configure/intelligence/ai-agents/delivery-channels). This page is where you watch them in action.

## Supported channels

| Channel | What it delivers | Setup |
|---------|------------------|-------|
| Email | Reports, period summaries, critical signals, daily digests. | Loops integration. |
| Slack | Real-time signal alerts, daily digest, on-demand reports. | Webhook. |
| Microsoft Teams | Same as Slack. | Webhook. |
| In-app | Everything (always on). | No setup. |
| Webhook | Plain JSON post for custom integrations. | Endpoint URL plus signing secret. |

## Routing rules

Each channel can be filtered by:

- Severity: only critical, only critical and high, or all.
- Engagement scope: all engagements, specific portfolios, or specific tags.
- Audience role: service owners, ops, leadership, customers.
- Quiet hours: hold non-critical messages outside business hours.

## Reliability

| Concern | Handling |
|---------|----------|
| Bounces or failures | Logged in [Notifications](/platform/communicate/notifications) and retried with backoff. |
| Rate limits | Per-channel limits, with overflow rolled into the next digest. |
| Repeat suppression | A 24-hour window prevents re-sending the same signal. |
