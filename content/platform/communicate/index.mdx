# Communicate

Communicate is where data leaves DigitalCore: to customers, executives, inboxes, and client portals. It is the COMMUNICATE [capability](/concepts/capabilities) made visible.

## What is in this section

| Page | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| [Reports](/platform/communicate/reports) | Scheduled and one-off reports: period summaries, executive briefs, QBRs. |
| [Client Portals](/platform/communicate/client-portals) | Curated views shared with customers. |
| [Notification Channels](/platform/communicate/notification-channels) | Email, Slack, Microsoft Teams, in-app: where messages are delivered. |
| [Summary Library](/platform/communicate/narrative-library) | Reusable phrases and tone settings DigitalCore draws on. |
| [Notifications](/platform/communicate/notifications) | Live stream of what was sent and what bounced. |

## How it works

Communicate sits on top of two services:

1. **Summary writing.** DigitalCore writes period summaries, executive reports, and report sections.
2. **Delivery.** Email, Slack and Teams webhooks, in-app, and PDF export.

Both follow the [agent profiles and policies](/platform/configure/intelligence/ai-agents) you set in Configure.
