# Client Portals

A client portal is a customer-facing window into part of an engagement. Customers log in, read their KPIs, view their reports, and see their cost stack, without you exporting screenshots into slide decks.

## What is shared

For each portal, you choose:

| Element | Default |
|---------|---------|
| KPIs | All performance KPIs marked customer-visible. |
| Period reports | Latest finalised report for the engagement. |
| Cost transparency | Optional: full, summary, or hidden. |
| SLA breach history | Visible on customer-visible KPIs. |
| Roadmap and commitments | If kept in priorities or decision cases. |

You can flip any item between visible, hidden, and summary-only at any time.

## Branding

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| Logo | Customer logo plus your logo. |
| Colour palette | Customer-aligned or DigitalCore default. |
| Domain | `customer.your-portal.digitalcore.app`. |
| Footer | Your contact info. |

## Access control

- Per-user invites with roles (viewer, power user).
- Multi-factor authentication required by default.
- Activity log: who logged in and what they viewed.
- Configurable session timeout.

Portal users live in a separate user space from your internal users. They cannot see anything beyond what the portal exposes.
