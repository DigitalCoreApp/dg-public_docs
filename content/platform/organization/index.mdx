# Organization

This section is where you manage the people in your DigitalCore workspace. That includes your team, your delivery partners, customer contacts, and the structure that ties them together.

## What's in this section

| Page | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| [Users](./users) | Internal users with access to DigitalCore. |
| [Partners](./partners) | External delivery partners and vendors. |
| [Stakeholders](./stakeholders) | Customer contacts and portal users. |
| [Structure](./structure) | Practices, units, teams, and regions. |
| [App Roles](./app-roles) | What each role can see and do. |

## How access works

Every person belongs to your organisation. People only see data for their organisation.

| Person type | Access |
|-------------|--------|
| User | Full access based on their role. |
| Partner | Reference only. No login. |
| Stakeholder | Optional [client portal](/platform/communicate/client-portals) login. |
