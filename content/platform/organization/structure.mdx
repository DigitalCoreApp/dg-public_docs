# Structure

Use Structure to mirror your internal organisation in DigitalCore: practices, business units, teams, regions. It is optional, but useful for rollups and access scoping.

## What you can model

| Concept | Example |
|---------|---------|
| Business unit | Public Sector, Enterprise, SMB. |
| Practice | Cloud, Data, Cybersecurity, Advisory. |
| Region | EMEA, NA, APAC. |
| Team | Cloud Migration Team Helsinki. |

You can build one hierarchy, several in parallel (such as by-practice and by-region), or skip it entirely.

## What it affects

| Surface | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Portfolio | Add "by practice" or "by region" grouping. |
| Users | Limit users to engagements in their unit. |
| Reports | Slice executive briefings by unit. |
| Compare | Group [Compare](/platform/portfolio/compare) by unit. |

## When to invest

If your org has clear, stable groupings that show up in every conversation, invest. If groupings shift constantly, skip it and use [service tags](/platform/configure/service-setup/service-tags) for ad-hoc grouping.
