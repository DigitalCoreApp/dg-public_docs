# Decisions

Decisions is where signals turn into action. It tracks the cases you open, the priorities you set, and the AI-generated scenarios that lay out options before you commit.

## What is in this section

| Page | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| [Decision Cases](/platform/decisions/cases) | The single container for a decision: AI agent inputs, risks, issues, decisions, follow-ups, and scenarios. |
| [Priorities](/platform/decisions/priorities) | Strategic outcomes you have committed to. See what is supporting or blocking them. |
| [Scenarios](/platform/decisions/scenarios) | AI-generated option sets inside a case, with assumptions and projected impact. |
| [AI Use Cases](/platform/decisions/ai-use-cases) | Recurring decision patterns the assistant has learned for your organisation. |

## How decisions flow

```
Signal noticed
   v
Case opened (manual or automatic)
   v
AI agent inputs, linked risks, and issues attached
   v
Scenario generated (options + assumptions)
   v
Review (compare options, adjust assumptions)
   v
Approve & Apply (data updates, follow-ups created)
   v
Outcome reviewed weeks or months later
```

The outcome review feeds back into DigitalCore's pattern memory so future scenarios get sharper.

## Approve and Apply

A scenario option moves through four states: **Proposed**, **Approved**, **Declined**, or **Applied**. Approval is a governance moment; Apply is a data moment. Splitting them lets you sign off in advance and apply on a future date. See [Scenarios](/platform/decisions/scenarios).
