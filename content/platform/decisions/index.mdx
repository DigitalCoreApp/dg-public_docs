# Decisions

Decisions is where signals turn into action. It tracks the cases you open, the priorities you set, and the AI-generated Responses that lay out options before you commit.

## The v5 vocabulary

DigitalCore is moving to clearer terms. Older terms still appear during the transition.

| Old term | New term |
|----------|----------|
| Scenario | Response |
| Variant | Option |
| Driver | Assumption |
| Commit | Approve & Apply |

A Response moves through four states: Proposed, Approved, Declined, or Applied. See [Response Frame](/platform/decisions/response-frame) for details.

## How decisions flow

```
Signal noticed
   v
Case opened (manual or automatic)
   v
Response generated (options + assumptions)
   v
Review (compare options, adjust assumptions)
   v
Approve & Apply (data updates, follow-ups created)
   v
Outcome reviewed weeks or months later
```

The outcome review feeds back into DigitalCore's pattern memory so future Responses get sharper.
