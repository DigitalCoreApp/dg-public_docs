# Exchange Rates

If your contracts run in different currencies but you report in one, this is where you keep the rates DigitalCore uses to convert.

## How rates are applied

A finance line is converted using the rate for that line's period month. So January 2026 stays at January's rate even as rates change later. Historical reports stay stable.

## How to enter rates

| Source | Notes |
|--------|-------|
| Manual | Enter rates per currency pair per month. |
| Imported file | Upload a CSV of rates. Planned. |
| Market feed | Pull from a data provider. Planned. |

Most customers use manual entry. Reports rarely need daily rates.

## Reporting currency

Your organisation has one reporting currency, set in [Account](/platform/configure/system/account). [Portfolio](/platform/portfolio) and [Pulse](/platform/pulse) totals use it.

A single engagement or contract view can show numbers in its own currency instead.
