# Integrations

Integrations bring data into DigitalCore from systems you already use and send data out to the channels where you communicate.

## Available today

| Integration | Direction | Purpose |
|-------------|-----------|---------|
| QuickBooks | In | Pull finance actuals automatically. |
| Microsoft Teams | Out | Delivery target for signals and summaries. |
| Slack | Out | Delivery target for signals and summaries. |
| Email | Out | Reports, summaries, notifications. |
| Webhooks | Out | Send events to any HTTPS endpoint. |
| AI assistants | Two-way | Natural-language access from Claude, Cursor, or ChatGPT. See [Integrations: AI assistants](/integrations/mcp). |

## Connect QuickBooks

1. Click **Connect QuickBooks**.
2. Sign in to QuickBooks Online and authorise.
3. Map QuickBooks accounts to your [Finance library](/platform/configure/data/library).
4. Choose a sync schedule (daily or hourly).

The Activity tab shows each sync run and any mapping errors.
