# Configure

Configure is where admins set up the rules DigitalCore plays by. Most screens you visit a few times a quarter. The exception is the Intelligence group (Thresholds, AI Agents, Policies), which you tune as your maturity grows.

## Intelligence

The trio that controls automation.

| Page | Sets |
|------|------|
| [Thresholds](/platform/configure/intelligence/thresholds) | What counts as a signal worth flagging. |
| [AI Agents](/platform/configure/intelligence/ai-agents) | Which assistants are running and what they detect. |
| [Policies](/platform/configure/intelligence/policies) | What those assistants are allowed to do. |

See the [Intelligence overview](/platform/configure/intelligence) for how to tune them as a unit.
