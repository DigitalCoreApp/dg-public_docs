# QuickBooks Integration

Connect QuickBooks to pull invoices and expenses into DigitalCore as actuals.

## What syncs

- **Invoices.** Pulled in as revenue actuals on the matching engagement.
- **Expenses.** Pulled in as cost actuals (matched by P&L category).

## Setup

1. Go to **Configure**, then **Integrations**.
2. Click **Connect QuickBooks**.
3. Sign in to QuickBooks and approve the connection.
4. Pick the company file.
5. Map QuickBooks classes (or customers) to DigitalCore engagements.
6. Map QuickBooks accounts to DigitalCore P&L categories.

## Sync schedule

DigitalCore polls QuickBooks once an hour. You can also click **Sync now**.

## Mapping

- **Engagements.** Use QuickBooks classes if you have them. Otherwise use customers.
- **P&L categories.** Map the chart of accounts you actually use. Skip rare categories.

## Tips

- Run the first sync against one engagement to confirm the mapping is right.
- Keep QuickBooks classes and DigitalCore engagement names aligned.
- For non-engagement expenses, leave them unmapped. They stay in QuickBooks only.

## Disconnect

Click **Disconnect** in the same panel. Past synced data stays. New data stops flowing.
