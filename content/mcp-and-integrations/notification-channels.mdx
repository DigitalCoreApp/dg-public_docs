# Notification Channels

DigitalCore can deliver reports and alerts to three places: email, Slack, and Microsoft Teams.

## What gets sent

- **Scheduled reports.** Monthly or quarterly executive summaries.
- **Real-time alerts.** Health colour changes, missed SLA terms, big variances.
- **Decision updates.** When a Decision moves status.

## Where things go

| Type | Email | Slack | Teams |
|---|---|---|---|
| Scheduled reports | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Alerts | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Decision updates | Yes | Optional | Optional |
| AI assistant chat | No | No | No |

## Set them up

- [Email](/mcp-and-integrations/email-notifications)
- [Slack](/mcp-and-integrations/slack-notifications)
- [Microsoft Teams](/mcp-and-integrations/teams-notifications)

## Per-user vs per-channel

- **Email** is per user. Each person picks what they get in their profile.
- **Slack and Teams** are per channel. Anyone in the channel sees the message.

## Tips

- Send executive summaries to a leadership channel.
- Keep alerts in a dedicated ops channel so they are not lost in chatter.
- Use email for personal alerts (Decisions assigned to you).
