# Free MCP

Free MCP gives any AI assistant access to DigitalCore's playbooks, KPI definitions, and a quick health-check tool. No account required.

## What you get

- 35 playbooks (margin recovery, capacity planning, SLA response, and more).
- KPI explainers (what a KPI means, how to calculate it, target ranges).
- A 5-input service health check.
- Email signup tool (optional).

## What you do not get

Live access to your DigitalCore data. For that, set up the [DigitalCore MCP](/mcp-and-integrations/paid-mcp).

## Setup

The endpoint is `https://mcpfree.digitalcore.app`.

### Claude Desktop

1. Open Claude Desktop and go to Settings, then Connectors.
2. Add a new connection.
3. URL: `https://mcpfree.digitalcore.app`.
4. Save and restart Claude.

### ChatGPT

Use the DigitalCore App from the GPT Store. It uses Free MCP.

### Cursor and other clients

Refer to your client''s documentation. Most accept a URL and no key.

## Tips

- Try asking, "Give me a service margin playbook for a managed services contract".
- Ask, "Explain First Response Time and what is a healthy target".
