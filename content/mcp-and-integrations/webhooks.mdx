# Webhooks (Planned)

Webhooks are on the roadmap. They will let you receive events from DigitalCore and trigger external actions.

## Planned events

- Engagement health changes.
- Decisions opened, approved, or applied.
- Missed SLA term recorded.
- Monthly check-in completed.
- Executive summary finalised.

## Planned format

A signed POST to your endpoint with a JSON payload. Standard fields include event type, organisation, timestamp, and the event-specific body.

## In the meantime

You can poll the [paid connection](/mcp-and-integrations/paid-mcp) on a schedule for similar results.

## Vote on this feature

Email feedback@digitalcore.app to share your use case. We prioritise webhooks against other roadmap items based on customer need.
